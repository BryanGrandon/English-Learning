## What are nouns

A **noun** is a word used to name **a person, animal, place, thing, event, or idea**.

📌 _Examples._

- teacher
- dog
- ball
- table
- house
- party
- happiness

Nouns are very important because they help identify **what or who we are talking about in a sentence**.

📌 _Examples._

- The **teacher** is in the classroom.

---

## **_Types of Nouns_**

Nouns can refer to different kinds of things.

🙎🏻‍♂️

_People._

- teacher
- doctor
- student

📌 _Examples._

- The **teacher** is speaking.

🐕

_Animals._

- dog
- cat
- bird

📌 _Examples._

- The **dog** is sleeping.

🌃

_Place._

- city
- house
- school

📌 _Examples._

- The **school** is big.

📒

_Things / Objects._

- table
- phone
- book

📌 _Examples._

- The **book** is on the table.

💡

_Ideas (Abstract nouns)._

- happiness
- love
- freedom

📌 _Examples._

- **Happiness** is important.

## Singular and Plural Nouns

A noun can be **singular** (one) or **plural** (more than one).

| Singular | Plural  |
| -------- | ------- |
| car      | cars    |
| house    | houses  |
| pen      | pens    |
| camera   | cameras |

Most plural nouns are formed by adding -s at the end of the word.

---

## Plural Rules

**_Add s_**

Most nouns form the plural by adding **-s**.

🏗️ _Structure._

- noun + s

📌 _Examples._

- car → cars
- house → houses
- pen → pens
- book → books

**_Consonant + y → ies_**

If a noun ends in **consonant + y**, change **y** to **ies**.

🏗️ _Structure._

- consonant + y → ies

📌 _Examples._

- party → parties
- city → cities
- baby → babies
- country → countries

⚠️ If the word ends in **vowel + y**, just add **-s**.

📌 _Examples._

- boy → boys

**_Add es_**

If a noun ends in **s, ss, sh, ch, x, or o**, add **-es**.

🏗️ _Structure._

- noun + es

📌 _Examples._

- bus → buses
- box → boxes
- watch → watches
- dish → dishes
- tomato → tomatoes

**_f / fe → ves_**

Some nouns ending in **f** or **fe** change to **ves** in the plural.

🏗️ _Structure._

- f / fe → ves

📌 _Examples._

- leaf → leaves
- wife → wives
- life → lives
- wolf → wolves

---

## **_Irregular Plural Nouns_**

Some nouns have **irregular plural forms** and do not follow a rule.

| Singular | Plural   |
| -------- | -------- |
| man      | men      |
| woman    | women    |
| child    | children |
| person   | people   |
| tooth    | teeth    |
| foot     | feet     |
| mouse    | mice     |

---

## **_Common Mistakes_**

**_Forgetting the plural s_**

- ❌ two car
- ✅ two **cars**

---

**_Wrong plural form_**

- ❌ childs
- ✅ **children**

---

**_Using plural with uncountable nouns_**

- ❌ informations
- ✅ **information**
