## What are proper nouns

**Proper nouns** are the **specific names** of people, places, events, organizations, or things. They always start with a **capital letter**.

📌 _Examples._

- **Sam Wilson**
- **Chicago**
- **February**

📌 _Example Sentence._

- **Maria** lives in **New York**.

## Capitalization Rule

Proper nouns **always begin with a capital letter**.

📌 _Examples._

- ❌ john lives in paris.
- ✅ **John** lives in **Paris**.

This rule applies to **names, places, organizations, languages, and events**.

## Types of Proper Nouns

🙎🏻‍♂️ _People's Names._

- Mary Garcia
- Alex Jones
- Mr. Henry Johnson

📌 _Examples._

- **Mary** is my teacher.

📅 _People's Names._

- January
- September
- Monday

📌 _Examples._

- My birthday is in **September**.

🎄 _Holidays._

- Christmas
- Easter
- Thanksgiving

📌 _Examples._

- We celebrate **Christmas** in December.

🏢 _Companies and Organizations._

- Starbucks
- Fiat
- United Nations

📌 _Examples._

- **Starbucks** is very popular.

🌆 _Countries, Cities and Towns._

- Argentina
- France
- New York

📌 _Examples._

- She lives in **France**.

🇨🇱 _Nationalities and Languages._

- English
- Japanese
- French

📌 _Examples._

- I speak **English**.

🌐 _Geographical and Celestial Names._

- the Pacific Ocean
- the North Pole
- Saturn

📌 _Examples._

- **Saturn** is a planet.

🛠️ _Monuments and Buildings._

- the Eiffel Tower
- the Colosseum
- the White House

📌 _Examples._

- The **White House** is in Washington.

📜 _Historical Events._

- World War II
- the Industrial Revolution
- Black Friday

📌 _Examples._

- **World War II** ended in 1945.

👨🏻‍🏫 _Titles of People._

- President Obama
- Doctor Norris
- General Alex

📌 _Examples._

- **Doctor Smith** is very famous.

⛪ _Religions and Sacred Texts._

- Catholicism
- Islam
- the Bible

📌 _Examples._

- **Islam** is a major religion.

🎞️ _Titles of Books, Movies and Art._

- The Godfather
- Gone with the Wind
- To Kill a Mockingbird

📌 _Examples._

- **The Godfather** is a famous movie.

## Common Mistakes

**_Not using capital letters_**

- ❌ i live in paris
- ✅ **I live in Paris**

---

**_Capitalizing common nouns_**

- ❌ I bought a **Car**
- ✅ I bought a **car**

---

**_Forgetting capitalization for languages_**

- ❌ i speak english
- ✅ I speak **English**
