## What is the verb _to be_?

The verb **to be** means **ser / estar** in Spanish and is **one of the most important verbs in English**. It can be used as a **main verb** or as an **auxiliary verb**.

📌 _Examples._

- He **is** my friend.
- They **were** at school.
- The book **is** on the table.

## **_Affirmative Form (Present Simple)_**

| Subject | Verb | Short Form |
| ------- | ---- | ---------- |
| I       | am   | I'm        |
| You     | are  | You're     |
| He      | is   | He's       |
| She     | is   | She's      |
| It      | is   | It's       |
| We      | are  | We're      |
| They    | are  | They're    |

📌 _Examples._

- I **am** a student.
- She **is** happy.
- They **are** at home.

---

## Negative Form

| Subject | Verb    | Short Form                |
| ------- | ------- | ------------------------- |
| I       | am not  | I'm not                   |
| You     | are not | You're not / You aren't   |
| He      | is not  | He's not / He isn't       |
| She     | is not  | She's not / She isn't     |
| It      | is not  | It's not / It isn't       |
| We      | are not | We're not / We aren't     |
| They    | are not | They're not / They aren't |

📌 _Examples._

- I **am not tired**.
- She **is not sad**.
- They **are not at home**.

---

## Interrogative Form

In questions with **to be**, the **verb comes before the subject**.

Question

- Am I?
- Are you?
- Is he?
- Is she?
- Is it?
- Are we?
- Are they?

📌 _Examples._

- **Are you tired?**
- **Is she a teacher?**
- **Are they at home?**

---

## Uses of To Be

| Use                            | Description                                    | Examples                 | Example Sentence              |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| **With Nouns**                 | Used to describe identity or profession        | teacher, doctor, student | I **am a teacher**.           |
| **With Adjectives**            | Used to describe states or characteristics     | happy, tired, hungry     | We **are happy**.             |
| **With Prepositions of Place** | Used to describe location                      | in, on, at               | The book **is on the table**. |
| **As an Auxiliary Verb**       | Helps form verb tenses like Present Continuous | is studying, are working | She **is studying** English.  |

---

## Common Mistakes

**_Missing the verb_**

- ❌ She happy.
- ✅ She **is** happy.

**_Wrong verb form_**

- ❌ She **are** happy.
- ✅ She **is** happy.

**_Wrong question order_**

- ❌ You are happy?
- ✅ **Are you happy?**
