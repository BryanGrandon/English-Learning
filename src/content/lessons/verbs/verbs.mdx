## What are Verbs?

A **verb** is the part of a sentence that expresses an **action**, **state**, or **existence** of the subject.

📌 _Examples._

- He **runs**.
- We **like** music.
- She **is** my friend.

In these sentences, the verbs show **what the subject does or is**.

---

## Types of Verbs

| Type                | Description                                             | Examples                                          | Example Sentence             |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Action Verbs**    | Describe an action that someone or something performs   | run, eat, study, play                             | She **runs** every morning.  |
| **State Verbs**     | Describe a state or condition, not a physical action    | be, know, like, believe                           | I **know** the answer.       |
| **Auxiliary Verbs** | Help form verb tenses, negatives, or questions          | be, do, have                                      | She **is studying** English. |
| **Modal Verbs**     | Express ability, possibility, permission, or obligation | can, could, may, might, must, should, will, would | She **can play** the piano.  |

---

## Verb Position in Sentences

In English, verbs usually appear **after the subject**.

🏗️ _Structure._

- subject + verb + object

📌 _Examples._

- She **likes** coffee.
- They **play** football.
- I **study** English.

---

## Short Forms (Contractions)

English commonly uses **short forms** instead of the full verb form.

| Full Form | Short Form |
| --------- | ---------- |
| I am      | I'm        |
| You are   | You're     |
| He is     | He's       |
| She is    | She's      |
| We are    | We're      |
| They are  | They're    |

📌 _Examples._

- I'm happy.
- She's my friend.

---

## Common Mistakes

**_Missing the verb_**

- ❌ She happy.
- ✅ She **is** happy.

**_Wrong verb order_**

- ❌ She coffee likes.
- ✅ She **likes coffee**.

**_Forgetting auxiliary verbs_**

- ❌ She studying English.
- ✅ She **is studying** English.
