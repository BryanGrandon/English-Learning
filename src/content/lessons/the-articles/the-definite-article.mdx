# The Definite Article

Aprende a usar **the** para hablar de personas, cosas y lugares concretos.

---

## Definite Article: **the**

Utilizamos **the** delante de un sustantivo cuando la persona, la cosa o el lugar son concretos o ya son conocidos tanto por el hablante como por el oyente.

Equivalent to: **el, la, los, las** (Spanish)

<section className='grid grid-cols-1 gap-4 sm:grid-cols-2'>
<LessonCard icon='example' title='Example'>

- The boy → el niño
- The book → el libro
- The girls → las niñas

</LessonCard>

<LessonCard icon='important' title='Important' background='#f97316'>

In English, **the** _never changes_

- #### It is the same for:
  - singular & plural
  - masculine & feminine

</LessonCard>
</section>

---

## Position Rule

El artículo **the** va antes del sustantivo. Si hay un adjetivo, **the** va antes del adjetivo.

<section className='grid grid-cols-1 gap-4 sm:grid-cols-2'>
<LessonCard icon='structure' title='Structure'>

> the + adjective + noun

</LessonCard>

<LessonCard icon='example' title='Example'>

- the car
- the red car
- the new teacher

</LessonCard>
</section>

## Uses of **the**

<section className='grid grid-cols-1 gap-4 lg:grid-cols-2'>
<LessonCard title='When something is specific' icon="focus" type="topic">
Úsalo cuando tanto el hablante como el oyente sepan exactamente de qué se está hablando.

- What is the name of the restaurant?
- Where is the bathroom?

</LessonCard>

<LessonCard title='When something was mentioned before' icon="reference" type="topic">
Úsalo cuando ya se haya mencionado un sustantivo.

- I saw a dog.
- The dog was very big.

</LessonCard>

<LessonCard title='When something is unique' icon="unique" type="topic">
Úsalo cuando solo haya uno de algo.

- the sun
- the moon
- the president

</LessonCard>
<LessonCard title='When describing a specific person or thing' icon="identify" type="topic">
Utiliza el artículo **the** para identificar a una persona o cosa concreta dentro de un grupo.

- The boy with the red shirt
- The teacher in my class
- The house on the corner

</LessonCard>
</section>

## Common Mistakes

<section className='grid grid-cols-1 gap-4 lg:grid-cols-2'>

<LessonCard title='Using the for general things' icon="mistake" type="topic" background='#f00'>

- ❌ The dogs are friendly (general idea)
- ✅ Dogs are friendly

</LessonCard>

<LessonCard title='Forgetting the when something is specific' icon="mistake" type="topic" background='#f00'>

- Forgetting the when something is specific
  - ❌ Where is bathroom?
  - ✅ Where is **the** bathroom?

</LessonCard>

</section>
