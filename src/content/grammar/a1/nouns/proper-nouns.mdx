# Proper Nouns

A **proper noun** is the name of a specific **person**, **place**, **thing**, or **organization**. Proper nouns _always begin with a capital letter_.

<ContentGrid>
<Callout type="example">

- Maria
- London
- Chile
- Google

</Callout>

<Callout type="example_sentences">

- ❌ john lives in paris.
- ✅ **_John_** lives in **_Paris_**.
- ❌ i study english.
- ✅ I study **_English_**.

</Callout>
</ContentGrid>

<Callout type="important">

Always use a **capital letter** for **_proper nouns_**.

</Callout>

---

## Types of Proper Nouns

Proper nouns can refer to many different kinds of names.

<ContentGrid>

<SectionCard title='Using capital letters'>

- People's Names
- Countries, Cities, and Towns
- Days and Months
- Holidays and Events
- Companies and Organizations
- Nationalities and Languages
- Rivers, Mountains, Oceans, and Planets
- Monuments and Buildings

</SectionCard>

<Grammar title='Capital letters' type="common_mistake">
- #### Not using capital letters
  - ❌ i live in paris
  - ✅ **I live in Paris**
- #### Capitalizing common nouns
  - ❌ I bought a **Car**
  - ✅ I bought a **car**
- #### Forgetting capitalization for languages
  - ❌ i speak english
  - ✅ I speak **English**

</Grammar>
</ContentGrid>
