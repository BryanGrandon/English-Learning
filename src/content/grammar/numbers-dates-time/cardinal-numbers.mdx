# Cardinal Numbers

Cardinal numbers are used to _count people and things_ and **express quantity**.

<LessonColumns className='sm:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-3'>
<LessonBlock icon='example' title='Examples' container="section" heading="p" >
  - one
  - two
  - three
  - ten
  - twenty
  - one hundred
</LessonBlock>

<LessonBlock title='Used For' icon="usage" container="blockquote" heading="p">

- Age.
- Dates.
- Prices.
- Quantities.
- Phone numbers.

</LessonBlock>

<LessonBlock title='Example Sentences' icon="example" container="section" heading="p">
  - I have **three** books.
  - She is **twenty** years old.
  - We bought **two** tickets.
  - The book costs **fifteen** dollars.
</LessonBlock>
</LessonColumns>

## Hundreds, Thousands, Millions

**_Hundreds_**

- one hundred
- two hundred

**_With "and"_**

- one hundred **and** twenty
- three hundred **and** fifty-five

**_Thousands_**

- one thousand
- ten thousand

**_Millions_**

- one million

---

## Tips

- Use **"and"** between hundreds and tens
- Use **hyphen (-)** for numbers like twenty-one
- Memorize 1–12 first
